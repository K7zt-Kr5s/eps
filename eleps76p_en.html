◎ It is board game for two people for iPhone, iPodTouch.
◎ About Eleps76+
Eleps was abbreviation of Electron Pieces, and the electron which can be added, distributed, arranged, moved and acquired as a common unit in any place time,76 pieces were prepared by having made it into the common piece supposing the piece of the character which can perform setup, acquisition, cooperation, and distribution in common, and the title name of Eleps76+ was attached as a game aiming at acquisition of the piece.
◎ The fundamental game method
・The player 1 is the first move.
・The player 1 piece is specified as the point with which the common piece on the board is specified, and the common piece is acquired.
・The number of the common piece becomes the score of player 1.
・Furthermore, four common pieces are specified as the non-specific point on the board.
・The player 2 also performs the same specification as the player 1 by player 2 piece and common piece.
・The above-mentioned specification is repeated and, finally the player with many scores becomes a victory.
◎ Function of Eleps 76+
・There are three kinds of pieces.
・The score setting of the board, with the numerical value and ? There are two kinds of fixed (1 to 8) and random (1 to 9).
・There are three kinds of thought methods of computer, Easy, Normal, Hard.
・There is a saving function.
◎ Composition of boards and pieces
Onthe board, there are 6 points with 2connecting point, 4 points with 3 connecting points, 6 points with 4connecting points, 2 points with 5 connecting points, 6 points with 5connecting points. Two players perform 9 times of the specification of four common pieces. There are 76 common pieces in total.
